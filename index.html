// PROCESS
How it works

Four steps from job description to download-ready resume.

STEP 01
📋

Paste the JD

Drop in the full job description — role, company, requirements. Any format works.

STEP 02
🧠

AI Analysis

Claude AI reads the JD, identifies key skills, technologies, and priorities.

STEP 03
✍️

Smart Tailoring

Resume content is reordered, rewritten, and reframed to match exactly what they want.

STEP 04
📄

PDF Export

Output matches original resume formatting — fonts, layout, spacing. Send it directly.

Built by a fresher,

Job hunting across multiple domains means rewriting your resume over and over. This tool automates that — so you spend time preparing for interviews, not reformatting PDFs.
